Top 20 Women's Day Eco Friendly Corporate Gift Ideas

What is International Women's Day and Its Significance

International Women's Day (IWD) is celebrated globally on March 8 every year to recognize the social, cultural, economic, and political achievements of women. The day also marks a call to action for accelerating gender equality. International Women's Day is a time to reflect on progress made, to celebrate the achievements of women and to empower and motivate women around the world. Women's Day was first celebrated in 1911, and since then, it has grown to become an important day for individuals and organizations to show their support for women's rights and equality.

Why Choose Eco-Friendly Gifts

As the world is becoming increasingly conscious about environmental sustainability, it is essential to consider the impact that our gifts and purchases have on the environment. Eco-friendly gifts are a great way to promote sustainable living while also giving a thoughtful gift that aligns with the recipient's values. When choosing eco-friendly gifts, it is essential to choose items that are not only environmentally friendly but also useful and practical.

20 Women's Day Eco-Friendly Corporate Gift Ideas :

1. Reusable Shopping Tote Bags - These bags are made from eco-friendly materials and can be used over and over again. Encourage eco-friendliness by gifting reusable shopping tote bags. They are stylish, durable, and can be used multiple times, reducing the need for single-use plastic bags.

2. Personalized Wooden Diary - A wooden diary is not only sustainable, but it also adds a touch of class to any desk. A personalized wooden diary is an excellent gift for women who love to journal. It's made of raw wood and can be customized to suit your recipient's preferences.

3. Seed Paper Notepad and Pen Set - This gift set is not only functional but also has the added benefit of planting wildflowers. The notepad is made of seed paper, which can be planted after use, and the pen has seeds that can be grown into beautiful plants later.
